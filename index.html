About us

We are the Jaiza team, students of NIS, consisting of Adilkhan, Miras, Daulet, Gibrat, Alikhan, and Dias. We have chosen a challenge focused on integrating the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) into the education system. To achieve this goal, we are using NASA data and developing a special website that will effectively address the set objectives.
